## üéÆ DETAILED GAME DESIGN

### Starting Point:
- **You:** Tiny food cart (5x5 pixels when small)
- **Goal:** Collect customers & resources to grow
- **Time Limit:** 5 minutes per level

### Collection Mechanics:

**What Sticks to You:**
- üë§ **Customers** (give you money passively)
- üí∞ **Coins** (direct currency)
- üì¶ **Resources** (ingredients, supplies)
- ‚ú® **Bonus items** (power-ups, multipliers)

**What Doesn't Stick (Obstacles):**
- üöó **Cars** (knock customers off)
- üè¢ **Buildings** (too big... until you're bigger!)
- ‚õî **Competitors** (actively avoid you... until you're bigger!)

### Growth Stages:

| Stage | Size | What You Collect | Visual |
|-------|------|------------------|--------|
| 1. Cart | Tiny | Individual customers, coins | Single cart sprite |
| 2. Stall | Small | Small groups, ingredients | Cart + table |
| 3. Shop | Medium | Larger crowds, resources | Small storefront |
| 4. Store | Large | Competitor carts! | Building facade |
| 5. Mall | Huge | Entire shops, buildings! | Multi-story |
| 6. Empire | MASSIVE | City blocks! | Skyscraper |

### Level Progression:

**Level 1: "Street Vendor"**
- Location: City sidewalk
- Goal: Collect 20 customers + $100
- Time: 3 minutes
- Obstacles: Pedestrians, cars

**Level 2: "Food Court King"**
- Location: Shopping mall
- Goal: Grow to Shop size
- Time: 5 minutes
- Obstacles: Security guards, other vendors

**Level 3: "Main Street Mogul"**
- Location: Downtown
- Goal: Collect 3 competitor carts
- Time: 5 minutes
- Obstacles: Traffic, rival businesses

**Level 4: "Block Buster"**
- Location: Business district
- Goal: Absorb 5 storefronts
- Time: 7 minutes
- Obstacles: Construction, delivery trucks

**Level 5: "Corporate Titan"**
- Location: Entire city
- Goal: Build empire (biggest possible)
- Time: 10 minutes
- Obstacles: Everything!

### Art Style:
- **Minimalist geometric** (Katamari-inspired)
- **Bright, colorful** (cheerful, not corporate)
- **Top-down 2D** (like classic arcade)
- **Particle effects** for collection
- **Size scaling** animation
- **Simple stick figures** for customers

### Sound Design (ZzFX):
- **Collection:** Satisfying "pop" when customer sticks
- **Growth:** Whoosh + chime when size increases
- **Coins:** Iconic coin sound
- **Obstacles:** Bonk when hitting things
- **Background:** Upbeat, simple melody

### Controls:

**Desktop/Laptop:**
- **Arrow keys / WASD:** Move direction
- **Space:** Speed boost
- **Q/E:** Quick turn

**Mobile:**
- **Virtual joystick:** Left side (movement)
- **Tap right side:** Speed boost
- **Two-finger:** Quick turn

**Gamepad:**
- **Left stick:** Movement
- **Right stick:** Fine control (Katamari-style!)
- **A button:** Boost

### Progression Systems:

**Unlocks:**
- New business types (bakery, tech startup, etc.)
- New cities/locations
- New customer types
- Power-ups (magnets, speed, double money)

**Challenges:**
- **Speed run:** Beat level in fastest time
- **Collector:** Get 100% of customers
- **Tycoon:** Biggest possible business
- **Efficient:** Fewest movements

**Leaderboards:**
- Biggest business per level
- Fastest completion
- Total customers collected
- Total money earned
